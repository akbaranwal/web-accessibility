Mawsmai Falls, situated at a short distance from Mawsynram, is a splendid waterfall emerging out of the hills. This has the distinction of being the fourth largest waterfall in India. It is also known as the Seven Sisters Waterfalls or Nohsngithiang Falls. It is a magnificent natural wonder. Water falls from a great height of 315 meters. It looks particularly beautiful during the time of sunset as the water is illuminated by vibrant colors of the setting sun. This is a spectacular sight.
